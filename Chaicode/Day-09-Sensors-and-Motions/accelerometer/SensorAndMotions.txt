What is a sensor?
A sensor is a harware component inside a device that detects and
measures physical properties from the real world.


Types of Sensors
1. Movement
2. Rotation
3. Light
4. Pressure
5. Steps Count
6. Device Orientation (landscape or portrait)
7. Magnetic Fields
